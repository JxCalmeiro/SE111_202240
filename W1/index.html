My Bio - All about me

I was born on January 5th 1990 in Providence, Rhode Island. I grew up in East Providence, Rhode Island and graduated from East Providence High School in 2008. I worked at Gamestop as a store manager from 2008-2013. After that I took a job with AT&T as a store manager from 2013-2018. In September of 2018 I left for Marine Corps Recurit Training upon Marine Corps Recruit Depot San Diego, California. After three months of recurit training, I countiued Marine Combat Training for one month at Camp Pendleton, California. Upon My completion of combat training I attended Aviation A&C School in Pensacola, Florida for six months. Then I continued my training at the Naval Aviaiton Tech Training in Virginia Beach, Virginia for three months. When my schooling was complete I was assgined my permanet duty sation with Marine Aviation Logistics Squadron - 11 in San Diego, California where I served as an Avionics Technician. In March 2021 I went on a deployment to Iwakuni, Japan until September 2021. After my deployment I spent the rest of my time in service with MALS-11 in San diego, California until September 2023. After my military service I moved back to Rhode Island where I am currently attending New England Institute of Technology, where I am obtaining a bachelors degree in Cyber Security and Network Engineering.
